About This Archive

This archive preserves the writings of Peter Lundin, M.D., a nephrologist who was also a dialysis patient. Dr. Lundin provided a unique perspective on dialysis and kidney disease, combining clinical expertise with personal experience. His articles were originally published at the Nephron Information Center.
