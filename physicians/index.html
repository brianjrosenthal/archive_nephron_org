The Nephrol Family

Interactive listserv with hundreds of physicians participating. High level discussions regarding variety of renal topics. For additional information about these discussion groups and other NKF computer-related activities go to CyberNephrology or The National Kidney Foundation.

  • NEPHROL - Covers general nephrology, all subjects including renal pathology and transplantation.
  • MDDIALYSIS - Specific for dialysis issues, intended for clinical nephrologists and medical directors.
  • NEPHJNL - nephrology/transplantation journal club.
  • NEPJHIST - history of renal medicine, ISN Video Legacy Project interview transcripts.
  • NEPHCNPT - discussions of individual clinical nephrology cases.
  • NEPHNPPT - discussions of individual renal biopsy cases.
  • NEPHDEVEL - discussion of humanitarian projects and ISN Sister Centers Program.
  • NEPHMIN - a stripped down version of NEPHROL-DIGEST with all repetition and North American-specific content removed. Designed for subscribers in developing countries.
  • KIDNEYDISEASE - an e-mail discussion group for patients with kidney diseases and for preESRD patients. Physicians and other practitioners are encouraged to join to volunteer helpful information.
  • NEPHROL-DIGEST
  • NEPHJNL-DIGEST
  • NEPJHIST-DIGEST
  • NEPHCNPT-DIGEST
  • NEPHNPPT-DIGEST
  • NEPHDEVEL-DIGEST
  • KIDNEYDISEASE-DIGEST
  • NEPHADMIN - e-mail discussion group for dialysis administrators - MDDIALYSIS patients automatically receive these posts.
