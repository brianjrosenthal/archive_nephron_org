🥗 Renal Nutrition

Comprehensive patient and professional education materials on nutrition for people with kidney disease. Created by registered dietitians, this collection covers essential topics for managing chronic kidney disease and dialysis through proper nutrition.

Topics include:
  • Sodium, Potassium, and Phosphorus management
  • Protein requirements for CKD patients
  • Personalized meal planning and lab values
  • Fiber, vitamins, and disaster preparedness
  • Resources in English and Spanish

🎥 40 Years of Hemodialysis

Historical lecture series by Stanley Shaldon, M.D., F.R.C.P., documenting the pioneering development of home hemodialysis in the 1960s. This 7-part video series, delivered in 2001, captures the remarkable history of the first practical dialysis machines, the AV fistula, and the concept of home dialysis.

Collection includes:
  • 7-part lecture series on the history of home dialysis
  • Video documentation of early dialysis techniques
  • Multiple formats: Quicktime (.mov), MPEG-4 (.mp4), and Windows Media (.wmv)
  • Historical context from a pioneering nephrologist
